# Top 10 SEO & AI Visibility Priorities for triangledehumidifiers.com

**Date:** March 17, 2026
**Based on:** Live site audit, SERP analysis, competitor comparison, AI visibility research
**Excludes:** GBP reviews (already in progress)

---

## The Core Problem

Your on-page SEO is actually strong — good schema, solid FAQs, real local content, decent word count. The issue is **off-site authority**. Your site isn't ranking for your core money keyword ("crawl space dehumidifier installation Raleigh NC") and — more concerning — isn't even appearing when someone searches your exact brand name + location. That means Google and AI models don't have enough external signals to trust and surface your site.

The priorities below are ordered by how much they'll move the needle, not by how easy they are.

---

## 1. Claim and Optimize Your Nextdoor Business Page

**Why this is #1:** Every one of your direct competitors — Crawl Space Ninja, Crawl Space Brothers, Crawlspace Medic, Triangle Crawl Space Solutions, Triangle Radiant Barrier — has a Nextdoor business page. You don't. Nextdoor is one of the highest-trust platforms for local home services, it generates direct referrals, and AI models pull from it. This is the single biggest gap between you and your competitors that you can close in 30 minutes.

**What to do:**
- Go to business.nextdoor.com and claim your page
- Add all services, service area (Holly Springs + surrounding cities), photos, and your phone number
- Ask a few of your existing customers to recommend you on Nextdoor (this is separate from Google reviews)

**Evidence:** Searched Nextdoor for dehumidifier services in Raleigh — 6 competitors have active pages. Triangle Dehumidifiers has zero presence.

---

## 2. Get Listed on Every Major Local Directory with Consistent NAP

**Why:** Your brand name search ("Triangle Dehumidifiers" Holly Springs NC) doesn't surface your site in the top 10 results. This means Google hasn't built a strong entity association for your business. Directory listings — with identical Name, Address, Phone across all of them — are how Google confirms a business is real and establishes entity recognition. They're also the documents AI models reference when answering "who does [service] in [city]?"

**Where to list (in priority order):**
1. Yelp (if not already claimed)
2. BBB (Better Business Bureau)
3. Angi / HomeAdvisor
4. Thumbtack
5. Houzz
6. Porch.com
7. Local Chamber of Commerce (Holly Springs, Fuquay-Varina)
8. NC contractor directories
9. MapQuest, Apple Maps, Bing Places

**Critical:** Use the exact same business name, address, and phone on every listing. "Triangle Dehumidifiers, LLC" — not "Triangle Dehumidifiers" on one and "Triangle Dehumidifiers LLC" on another.

**Evidence:** Brand name search returned competitors and unrelated businesses. No directory listings for Triangle Dehumidifiers appeared.

## 3. Submit Your Site to Bing Webmaster Tools and Verify Indexing

**Why:** Bing feeds data directly to ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, and Perplexity. If your site isn't properly indexed in Bing, you're invisible to the AI search engines that matter most. Most small businesses neglect Bing entirely, which means this is a free competitive advantage.

**What to do:**
- Go to bing.com/webmasters and add your site
- Submit your sitemap (https://www.triangledehumidifiers.com/sitemap-index.xml)
- Verify ownership
- Once indexed, check the AI Performance Report — this shows how often your site is cited in Bing AI/Copilot answers

**Evidence:** Could not confirm Bing indexing for Triangle Dehumidifiers. Your robots.txt correctly allows all crawlers (including AI bots), so the content is accessible — it just may not be submitted.

## 4. Create an About Page with Real E-E-A-T Signals

**Why:** Your site has no About page. No team bio. No credentials. No license number. No certifications. The only E-E-A-T signal is "15+ years of experience" on the homepage and Nathan's name in blog bylines. Google's quality rater guidelines and AI models both heavily weight authoritativeness for YMYL (Your Money, Your Life) topics — and home services involving mold and structural integrity qualify. Every competitor's page that ranks above you is building trust signals you're missing.

**What to include on /about:**
- Nathan Rider's background, training, and years in the industry
- Any certifications (EPA Lead-Safe, manufacturer training for Aprilaire/Santa Fe, BPI, etc.)
- Your NC contractor license number
- Insurance mention
- Why you started Triangle Dehumidifiers
- A real photo of you (or you + team on a job site)
- Your connection to the Holly Springs community

**Also:** Add a brief "About the Author" block to every blog post linking to this page. This strengthens the E-E-A-T chain across the site.

**Evidence:** Fetched all service pages and homepage — no About page exists. Navigation goes: Services → Service Areas → Blog → Contact. Competitor Glover Environmental features "35 years combined experience" prominently.

## 5. Fix Missing Meta Descriptions on Every Page

**Why:** Every page audited — homepage, Raleigh service page, mold treatment, whole-house dehumidifier, ERV installation — is missing a meta description. This means Google auto-generates your snippet from random page text. You're leaving click-through rate on the table. Pages with well-written meta descriptions get 5–10% higher CTR than auto-generated ones, and CTR is a ranking signal.

**What to do:** Write a unique meta description (under 155 characters) for all 20 pages. Each one should include: primary keyword, location, and a reason to click (free inspection, years of experience, locally owned, etc.)

**Example for homepage:**
> Crawl space dehumidifier installation in Raleigh & Wake County NC. Locally owned, 15+ years experience. Free inspections. Call (919) 867-0580.

**Example for mold treatment:**
> Crawl space mold removal in Raleigh NC. EPA-registered products + humidity correction to prevent regrowth. Free inspection — (919) 867-0580.

**Evidence:** WebFetch audit of 5 pages — none had a visible meta description.

## 6. Build Backlinks from Local Referral Partners (Not Directories — Real Partners)

**Why:** Your competitors who rank above you likely have stronger backlink profiles. Glover Environmental has two physical locations and years of link accumulation. Crawl Space Ninja is a franchise with national domain authority. You can't match their domain authority overnight, but you can earn the *right kind* of local links that carry outsized weight for local rankings.

**Highest-value link opportunities:**
- **Home inspectors:** Offer to be their recommended dehumidifier installer. A link from a local inspection company's "trusted partners" page is highly relevant.
- **Real estate agents:** Write a guest post for a local agent's blog about "what to look for in a crawl space when buying a home in Holly Springs." They get free content, you get a relevant backlink.
- **HVAC companies:** You don't compete directly. A referral partnership where you recommend each other (with links) benefits both.
- **Supplier/manufacturer sites:** If you install Aprilaire or Santa Fe, ask to be listed as a local installer on their dealer locator.
- **Local media:** A story in the Holly Springs Sun, Fuquay-Varina Towne Player, or WRAL about humidity problems in NC homes = a powerful local backlink.

**Evidence:** You're not ranking for your main keyword despite having better on-page content than some competitors who are ranking. The gap is almost certainly domain authority / backlinks.

## 7. Start a YouTube Channel with Short Job-Site Videos

**Why this matters for both SEO and AI:** YouTube transcripts are indexed by Google and ingested by AI models. A 2–3 minute video showing a dehumidifier installation, a moisture reading, or a before/after crawl space transformation creates content that: (a) ranks in YouTube search and Google video results, (b) gets embedded on your service pages for engagement signals, (c) provides transcript text that AI models cite, and (d) builds brand mentions in a format your competitors aren't using.

**Start with these 5 videos:**
1. "What's inside a crawl space with high humidity?" (walk-through with moisture meter)
2. "Crawl space dehumidifier installation — what to expect" (time-lapse of a real job)
3. "Do you need a dehumidifier or full encapsulation?" (you explaining the difference on camera)
4. "Signs your Raleigh home has a humidity problem" (visual tour of symptoms)
5. "Why newer NC homes still have moisture problems" (educational)

You don't need professional production. A phone, decent lighting, and your knowledge are enough. Aim for 2–3 minutes each.

**Evidence:** Zero YouTube results for "Triangle Dehumidifiers." No video content on the site. Competitors also mostly lack video — this is an open lane.

Research suggests AI models need roughly 20–30 quality documents about a topic to meaningfully associate a brand with that expertise. You need to consistently grow your content library — but with strategic topics, not random ones.

**Next 6 posts, in priority order:**

**Each post should link to at least 2 service pages** with natural anchor text.

**Evidence:** 5 blog posts vs. competitors with deeper content libraries. Thin topical authority signal for Google and AI models.

## 9. Answer Questions on Reddit About Humidity/Crawl Spaces in NC

**Why:** Reddit is one of the most-cited sources in AI-generated answers. When someone asks ChatGPT or Perplexity "who should I call for crawl space humidity in Raleigh," the AI pulls from Reddit threads, forum discussions, and review sites — not just your website. Building genuine, helpful presence on Reddit creates the kind of third-party mentions that both Google and AI models use to validate your expertise.

**Where to participate:**
- r/raleigh (local questions about home issues)
- r/homeimprovement (crawl space and moisture questions)
- r/homeowners (humidity, mold, insulation questions)
- r/HVAC (dehumidifier and ventilation questions)

**Rules:** Don't spam. Don't drop your link in every answer. Give genuinely helpful answers. Mention you're a contractor in the Triangle when it's natural. Over time, people will ask for recommendations and your name comes up — that's the kind of organic mention AI models pick up. Even 2–3 quality answers per week compounds fast.

**Evidence:** Searched Reddit + YouTube + Nextdoor for "Triangle Dehumidifiers" — zero results. Your brand has no off-site footprint beyond your own website.

## 10. Expand City Pages to Cover More Services Per City

**Why:** You have 5 city pages, all targeting "crawl space dehumidifier [city] NC." That's a good start, but you're leaving search volume on the table. Someone searching "mold treatment Holly Springs NC" or "whole house dehumidifier Cary NC" won't land on your site because those pages don't exist.

**Expansion plan:**
- For each of your 5 cities (Holly Springs, Raleigh, Cary, Apex, Fuquay-Varina), create landing pages for: mold treatment, encapsulation, whole-house dehumidifier, ERV installation
- That's 20 new city+service pages (5 cities × 4 additional services)
- Each must have genuinely unique local content — not templates with the city name swapped. Reference specific neighborhoods, housing stock, soil conditions, or climate factors per city.

**Don't do this all at once.** Start with the highest-volume combinations:
1. Crawl space mold treatment + each city (5 pages)
2. Crawl space encapsulation + each city (5 pages)
3. Then whole-house dehumidifier and ERV pages

**Evidence:** Sitemap shows city pages only exist for the crawl space dehumidifier service. Competitors like Triangle Radiant Barrier use this exact city × service page strategy and rank for dozens of local keyword combinations.

## What You Can Skip (For Now)

These are things AI tools commonly recommend that **won't move the needle** for your specific situation right now:

- **llms.txt file** — No major AI model uses these. Google has explicitly said they don't.
- **Complex schema experiments** — Your schema is already better than most competitors. Improving it further has diminishing returns.
- **Paid syndication / advertorials** — Not cost-effective for a local service business your size.
- **AI chatbots on your site** — Nice-to-have, not a ranking factor.
- **Social media posting** — Facebook/Instagram posts don't meaningfully impact SEO or AI visibility for local services. Reddit and YouTube do (see #7 and #9).
- **Core Web Vitals obsession** — Your Astro build is already fast. Micro-optimizing page speed beyond "good" doesn't move local rankings.
