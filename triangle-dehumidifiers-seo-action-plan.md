## Priority 1: Quick Wins (Do This Week)

### 1. Add Meta Descriptions to Every Page

The crawl found missing meta descriptions on key pages (homepage, Raleigh service page). Every page needs a unique, compelling meta description under 155 characters that includes your target keyword and a reason to click.

**Example for homepage:**
> Professional crawl space dehumidifier installation in Raleigh & Wake County, NC. Locally owned. Free inspections. Call (919) 867-0580.

### 2. Add an About/Team Page

You have zero E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust) signals beyond the author byline on blogs. AI models and Google both weigh this heavily. Create an `/about` page with:

- Nathan's background and years of experience
- Any certifications (EPA, NATE, BPI, manufacturer certifications)
- Why you started the business
- Photo of you and/or your team
- License number and insurance mention

### 3. Add Review/Testimonial Content to Page Bodies

Your schema shows 19 reviews at 5.0 stars, but the actual page content doesn't feature individual testimonials. Add 2–3 real customer quotes (with first name + city) to your homepage and top service pages. This helps both conversions and AI citation — LLMs pick up on social proof in body text.

## Priority 3: AI Search Visibility (GEO/AEO)

This is the newer frontier. AI models like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude pull from different signals than Google. Here's what to do:

### 7. Structure Content for LLM Extraction

You're already doing this well with FAQs and schema. Double down:

- **Write definitive answers in the first 1–2 sentences** of each section. LLMs extract concise, quotable statements.
- **Use the format: question as H2, direct answer immediately below, then supporting detail.** This mirrors how AI models parse content.
- **Add "What is [topic]?" definitions** at the top of service pages. LLMs love clear definitions they can cite.

### 8. Get Active on Reddit and YouTube

LLMs heavily cite Reddit (it's one of the top-cited domains in AI answers). YouTube transcripts also feed into AI training data.

- **Reddit:** Answer questions in r/homeimprovement, r/homeowners, r/HVAC, r/raleigh about crawl space and humidity topics. Don't spam — give genuinely helpful answers and mention your experience as a contractor in the Triangle. Over time, this builds the kind of mentions LLMs pick up.
- **YouTube:** Even short 2–3 minute videos showing crawl space inspections, before/after dehumidifier installs, or humidity meter readings. Transcripts get indexed and cited.

### 9. Get Listed on Relevant Directories and Third-Party Sites

AI models pull from aggregator sites. Make sure you're listed and have consistent NAP (name, address, phone) on:

- Google Business Profile (you're working on reviews — good)
- Yelp
- Angi (formerly Angie's List)
- HomeAdvisor
- BBB (Better Business Bureau)
- Nextdoor
- Local Chamber of Commerce
- NC contractor directories

Each listing is another "document" that teaches AI models about your brand.

### 10. Publish Data-Rich, Original Content

AI models skip generic content they've seen 50 versions of. What makes yours citable:

- **Your actual humidity readings** from local crawl spaces (e.g., "We typically see 75–85% RH in unsealed crawl spaces in Holly Springs during July")
- **Before/after data** from real installs
- **Local cost ranges** with specifics
- **NC building code references** (the 4 ACH50 threshold, etc. — even though that's more Peak Energy, cross-referencing builds authority)

## Priority 4: Technical & Backlink Improvements

### 11. Backlink Strategy (You Mentioned This — Good)

Beyond GBP reviews, prioritize these link sources:

- **Local news/media** — Pitch a story to WRAL, News & Observer, or Triangle Business Journal about humidity problems in NC homes. Even a single local news link is worth dozens of directory links.
- **Supplier/manufacturer pages** — If you install Aprilaire, Santa Fe, or other brands, ask to be listed as an authorized installer on their websites.
- **Home inspection companies** — Build referral relationships. A link from a local home inspector's "recommended contractors" page is relevant and valuable.
- **Real estate agent blogs** — Offer to write a guest post about "what to look for in a crawl space when buying a home in Raleigh." Agents love this content and will link to you.
- **Local business partnerships** — HVAC companies, pest control, waterproofing companies that don't directly compete but serve the same homeowners.

### 12. Technical SEO Checks

- **Verify canonical tags** are present on every page (the crawl couldn't confirm these)
- **Check page speed** — Run Lighthouse on your top 5 pages. Your Astro setup should be fast, but verify.
- **Confirm XML sitemap** is submitted in Google Search Console and Bing Webmaster Tools
- **Submit to Bing** specifically — Bing feeds data to ChatGPT and Copilot. Many small businesses neglect Bing.
- **Check mobile usability** in Search Console for any flagged issues

### 13. Internal Linking Improvements

Your pages link to each other, but you can strengthen the web:

- **Every blog post should link to at least 2 service pages** with descriptive anchor text (not "click here")
- **Service pages should link to related blog posts** — e.g., the dehumidifier page should link to the "signs of humidity problems" and "dehumidifier cost" posts
- **Add a "Related Services" section** at the bottom of each service page
- **City pages should link to the main service page AND to each other** — "We also serve nearby Cary" type links

## Priority 5: Ongoing Monitoring

### 14. Set Up Tracking

- **Google Search Console** — Monitor impressions, clicks, and average position weekly. Look for queries you're appearing for but not ranking well.
- **Bing Webmaster Tools** — Check the AI Performance Report (shows how often you're cited in Copilot/Bing AI answers)
- **Google Analytics** — Track which pages drive contact form submissions and calls
- **Manually test AI responses** — Monthly, ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude: "Who installs crawl space dehumidifiers in Raleigh NC?" and "What's the best crawl space dehumidifier company near Raleigh?" Track whether you show up.
